Worker app v1 — all screens

Eighteen screens consolidating the worker-app v1 design surface. Six approved core screens establish the brand anchors (shift list home, confirm arrival, shift in progress, confirm departure, seal moment, receipt card). Twelve supporting screens extend the visual language to authentication, SMS entry, supervisor pending, receipt history, error states, empty state, and the break flows. The receipt card is the ONE cream moment in the worker app — the printed-document anchor. Everywhere else: charcoal.
